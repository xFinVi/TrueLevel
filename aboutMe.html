About True Level Scaffolding

Wayne Burcombe O'dell

Welcome to True Level Scaffolding LTD, my name is Wayne Burcombe-O'Dell and I am the company's Director. I have been scaffolding for around 11 years and it has been a dream for a while to start my own company which became a reality in June 2022. We started running and haven't looked back. The last 6 months have been amazing and I cannot wait to see what the future brings in for us as a company.

Wayne is a CISRS qualified scaffolder and have been since 2014. I have been in the scaffolding industry for just over 11 years now and am able to erect many different scaffold structures I.e standard tower scaffolding around your house to beam work scaffolds plus more intricate scaffolding. I have experienced many different forms of scaffolding in our small time in the business and looking forward to expanding that knowledge.
